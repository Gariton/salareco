# Salareco Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Salareco (the "App") handles user information.

## 1. Information Handled by the App

The App handles the following information depending on what the user enters, selects, creates, or shares:

- Income source information: income source name, notes, display color, salary payment day, salary announcement day
- Salary and bonus records: target year and month, payment date, payment items, deduction items, amounts, notes
- Work hour information: work hour item names and hour values
- Template information: template name, payment items, deduction items, notes
- Images such as payslips that the user selects for photo import
- In-app purchase status
- App settings: launch authentication setting, sharing privacy settings, notification settings, and similar preferences
- Information that may be collected or processed by advertising providers in connection with ads shown in the free plan

The App is not designed to require users to enter their name, address, phone number, email address, contacts, location information, or health information as part of its core functionality. However, if a user voluntarily enters such information in notes, income source names, or other fields, that information will be stored as App data.

## 2. Purposes of Use

The App uses or processes information for the following purposes:

- Recording, calculating, and displaying salary, bonus, deduction, and work hour information
- Managing income sources and templates
- Syncing data between devices signed in with the same Apple ID using iCloud
- Displaying salary records in Home Screen widgets
- Sending local notifications for salary payment days, salary announcement days, and related reminders
- Assisting data entry by sending selected images to the OpenAI API for AI reading
- Exporting CSV files and sharing templates or salary records
- Verifying and restoring in-app purchases and providing Plus features
- Displaying ads in the free plan, measuring ad performance, and preventing fraud
- Investigating issues, responding to inquiries, and improving the service

## 3. Storage and Sync

Core data such as salary records, income sources, templates, and work hour information is generally stored on the user's device.

When iCloud and CloudKit are available, the data may be stored in Apple's iCloud private database to sync data between devices signed in with the same Apple ID. Use, storage, syncing, and deletion through iCloud are managed according to Apple's terms and privacy policy.

If iCloud is not available, the App falls back to local storage on the device.

## 4. Photo Import

The App's photo import feature reads only images that the user explicitly selects from the photo library. Selected images are sent to the OpenAI API so AI can read payment items, deduction items, amounts, and similar details. The App operator does not store the image itself on its own server for the photo import feature.

Data sent to the OpenAI API is handled according to OpenAI's terms, privacy policy, and data control documentation. If you do not want unnecessary personal or sensitive information in a payslip image to be sent, do not use the photo import feature.

Payment items, deduction items, amounts, and other data extracted from the photo are stored as salary record data on the device or in iCloud only if the user saves the record.

## 5. Biometric and Passcode Authentication

If enabled by the user, the App uses Face ID, Touch ID, or the device passcode to help protect salary data when the App launches.

The App does not collect, store, or transmit biometric information such as face or fingerprint data. Authentication is performed on the device using iOS LocalAuthentication.

## 6. Notifications

If the user allows notifications and sets a salary payment day or salary announcement day, the App schedules local notifications on the device.

Notification content may include the income source name and salary-related reminder text. Users can change or disable notifications at any time in the device notification settings.

## 7. Sharing and Export

The App allows users to share or export the following through their own actions:

- Salary record sharing links
- Template sharing links or shareable text
- Salary record CSV files

Sharing links and CSV files may include income source names, salary and bonus amounts, payment items, deduction items, work hours, notes, and other data selected by the user for sharing. Please review the content and privacy settings before sharing.

Sharing links encode the included information in the URL. Anyone who receives the link may be able to view the included information, so please handle shared links carefully.

## 8. Advertising

The free plan may display ads provided by Google AdMob. For ad delivery, performance measurement, and fraud prevention, Google or advertising providers may collect or process information such as device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, App usage information, and ad impression or click information.

Please review the relevant providers' privacy policies for details about how they handle information.

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses data for advertising: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

When the user purchases Plus features, banner ads inside the App are removed.

## 9. In-App Purchases

The App uses Apple's StoreKit to provide Plus features. Purchase processing, payment information, refunds, and purchase history are handled by Apple.

The App checks purchase status to determine whether Plus features are active, but it does not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers.

## 10. Sharing with Third Parties

The App operator does not sell or provide users' salary records or other personal information to third parties without user consent, except as required by law.

However, user information may be processed by third-party services in the following cases:

- Storage and sync through iCloud/CloudKit
- Image analysis for photo import through the OpenAI API
- Ad delivery through Google AdMob
- In-app purchases through Apple StoreKit
- When the user sends information to third parties using sharing features, CSV export, the operating system's share sheet, or similar functions

## 11. Data Deletion

Users can delete salary records, income sources, templates, and similar data inside the App.

If the App is deleted from the device, local App data stored on that device is deleted. If iCloud sync is enabled, deletion and sync behavior for iCloud data follow iCloud specifications and the user's iCloud settings.

Shared links, CSV files, screenshots, and data already sent to third parties may not be deleted from the recipient's environment even if the data is deleted from the App.

## 12. Security

The App uses standard iOS features, SwiftData, iCloud/CloudKit, LocalAuthentication, and similar technologies to help protect user data.

However, users should carefully manage situations under their own control, such as device loss, forwarded sharing links, CSV file storage locations, and sharing data with third-party apps.

## 13. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that a child has entered personal information into the App, please contact us using the contact information above.

## 14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to feature additions, legal changes, or changes to services used by the App. If there are material changes, we will notify users in the App or on the public policy page.
